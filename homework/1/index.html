Artificial Intelligence

Overview History Dangers Everyday Uses


Artificial intelligence refers to a computer being able to perform intelligent or human-like actions.

These actions include learning from experience, making rational decisions, and being able to reason.

While these machines are currently being focused on only performing specific tasks instead of simply being intelligent beings, artificially intelligent machines are capable of rivaling human experts in their fields.

Although artificial intelligence has proven to be incredibly useful in many different fields, it comes with its own risks as well.
